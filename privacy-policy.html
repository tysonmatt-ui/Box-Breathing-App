Mindful Breathing helps you regulate your autonomic nervous system through guided breathing exercises. The app sends gentle reminders throughout your day, prompting you to pause and take a few mindful breaths. BREATHING PATTERNS Choose from five scientifically-informed breathing patterns: - Calm (4-4-8) — Extended exhale activates your parasympathetic nervous system - Classic Box (4-4-4-4) — Equal phases for balance and focus - Relaxing (4-7-8) — Dr. Weil's technique for calm and sleep - Energizing (6-6) — Rhythmic breathing for alertness - Extended Calm (5-5-10) — Deep relaxation with longer cycles SMART REMINDERS Set your active hours and preferred frequency. The app delivers gentle reminders at random intervals, prompting you to pause and breathe. Works even when the app is closed. AMBIENT SOUNDS Enhance your practice with calming ambient sounds: Rain, Ocean Waves, Forest, or White Noise — all generated on-device with no downloads needed. TRACK YOUR PROGRESS Monitor your breathing journey with session counts, daily streaks, weekly activity, and milestone celebrations. Build a consistent mindfulness habit. VISUAL THEMES Choose from four beautiful dark themes: Midnight, Ocean, Forest, and Sunset. PRIVACY FIRST All data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Your breathing practice is yours alone. Developed by EvolveChain Apps.
